Welcome to MTGADraft!

Want to draft in MTGA with your friends? Noticed you currently can't do that natively? We're here for you!

  1. Enable Detailed logs in game, the toggle can be found in Options > View Account > Detailed Logs (Plugin Support), importing your collection won't work without this activated.
  2. Upload your MTGA logs (you should find them at C:\Users\YourName\AppData\LocalLow\Wizards Of The Coast\MTGA\output_log.txt) to restrict the draft to the cards you actually own in the game.
  3. Use the controls on top of the page to enter your User Name and indicate your preferences.
  4. Share your session ID with your friends (or join their own session) and click ready to start drafting!

Options

Personal options:

  • Language: Adjusts the display language of cards.
  • Card Order: Adjusts the order of cards within a sealed pool or your drafted cards.
  • Restrict to Collection: If unchecked, your collection will not limit the cards available. If every players unchecks this, you will draft using every cards. (Particularly useful if you don't intend on playing specifically on MTGA)
  • Session ID: A unique identifier for your session, you can use any name, just make sure to use the same as your friends to play with them!

Session options (Shared by everyone in your session):

  • Public: Flags your session as public. It will appear in the "Public Sessions" menu so anyone can directly join.
  • Set: Select a set to draft using only with cards from this set.
  • Boosters per Player: Defaults to 3 in draft and 6 in sealed.
  • Bots: Adds virtual players to your draft. They are pretty dumb, so their utility is limited, but they are doing their best :(
  • Pick Timer: Maximum time in seconds allowed to pick a card in each booster. Default is 60 seconds, 0 means the timer is disabled.
